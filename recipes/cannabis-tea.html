Tea Blend

  • 3/4 cup ceylon black tea leaves

Steps

Make the Cannabis Tincture

  5. Using a small funnel, pour tincture into four 2-ounce dark-glass dropper bottles. Tinture is ready to use immediately, although it will become more poten over time.
  6. Store tincture in the refrigerator for up to ine year

Make The Tea Blend

  1. Combine the tea ingredients and store in an airtight container until ready to use.
  2. To prepare a cup of tea, steep 1 tablespoon of tea blend in 1/2 cups of boiling water until infused, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in cannabis tincture to your taste and sweeted as desired.
